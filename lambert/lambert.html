LAMBERT
The Lambert Quasirandom Sequence.

LAMBERT is a FORTRAN90 library which implements the Lambert sequence in 1, 2, 3 or 4 dimensions.

The Lambert sequence is a sequence of points in M dimensions, contained in the unit hypercube, and expressible as dyadic fractions, that is, fractions whose denominator is a power of 2.

Drawbacks of the current implementation are that I don't really understand the underlying principle, so I can't extend it to higher dimensions, and I don't see how to "jump ahead" in the sequence and compute, say, the 137th point without computing the previous 136 values. It would also be nice to extend the sequence to using other fractional bases besides 2.
