BAYES_WEIGHT
  Simple Bayesian Statistics demonstrations.
 
  Suppose an event is a roll of a die.
  We have two dice to choose from, each with
  a different characteristic probability
  density function (PDF).  We know the form
  of both PDF's.
 
  For each event, the probability we will 
  choose die #1 or die #2 is W1 or W2.
  But W1 and W2 are unknown to us, and we must
  deduce them from the results of the rolls.
